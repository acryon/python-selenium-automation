How to Make the Best Homemade Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F. Spray a baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, water, and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan, bake for 40 to 48 minutes, and enjoy!
